# Pricing — Mytradelink

Mytradelink is a digital profile page builder for tradespeople. Start free, upgrade when you are winning more work. All prices in AUD and include GST. Cancel anytime.

## Free
- Price: A$0/month, forever
- Card required: No
- Limits: Shows a small Mytradelink badge in your page footer
- Features:
  - Public profile page at /t/your-name
  - Large Call and WhatsApp buttons
  - Photo gallery plus before / after photos
  - Certifications and Google reviews link
  - Banner image and 3 social links
  - Areas covered and payment methods
  - 5 theme accent colours

## Pro
- Price: A$15/month, or A$149/year (billed annually, saves A$31)
- Card required: Yes
- Limits: None of the Free limits; no Mytradelink badge
- Features:
  - Everything in Free
  - Quote request form with photo uploads
  - Email alerts on every quote
  - Emergency callout button (24/7)
  - Intro video at the top of your page
  - Custom theme accent colour
  - Mytradelink badge removed

## Notes
- Billing is handled by Stripe. Switch between monthly and yearly anytime from the dashboard.
- If you cancel, your page stays live on the Free plan; Pro-only sections are hidden until you upgrade again.
- Most tradies do not need a separate website; one Mytradelink page covers what most customers want.
