Tradies get more work by turning the attention they already have into calls. Most of the growth is not new advertising; it is closing the gap between someone finding you and someone contacting you. Get found, look trustworthy in one glance, and make calling you a single tap. Then quote fast and follow up.

Here are nine ways that work, roughly in order of effort versus payoff.

## 1. Turn the views you already get into calls

You are probably getting more views than you think, from word of mouth, your Google listing and your socials. The leak is at the contact step. If a customer cannot quickly see your work and tap to call, they ring the next tradie. A single [profile page](/sign-up) with photos, reviews and big Call and WhatsApp buttons fixes that leak before you spend a cent on more views.

## 2. Set up a Google Business Profile

It is free and it is where most local customers look first. Add photos, your trades, your areas and your hours. Then link your profile page from it so people can see more of your work.

## 3. Get reviews, and ask properly

Reviews are the single strongest trust signal you have. Ask every happy customer the day the job finishes, when they are most pleased. Send a direct link to your Google review page so it takes them ten seconds. A handful of recent five-star reviews wins more work than any slogan.

## 4. Quote fast

The tradie who replies first often wins, because the customer stops calling around once they have a clear price. Same-day quotes signal that you are organised. A [quote template](/tools/quote-template) lets you send a professional price in minutes; the full method is in [how to write a quote that wins the job](/blog/how-to-write-a-quote-that-wins-the-job).

## 5. Show your work

Photos of finished jobs, and before / after shots, do the selling for you. A customer who can see a tidy job like theirs is half sold before they call.

## 6. Make one link you can put everywhere

A single shareable profile link goes in your Google listing, your van signage, your text signature, your invoices and your social bios. One link, everywhere, all pointing customers to call you. That is the idea behind a [one-link profile for tradies](/blog/one-link-for-tradies-turn-views-into-jobs).

## 7. Follow up on quotes

A quote with no follow-up is a coin toss. A short, friendly message a couple of days later wins back a real share of jobs that would otherwise drift to whoever called.

## 8. Ask for referrals

Your existing customers know other people who need your trade. Tell them plainly you are taking on work and would appreciate them passing your link on. Most are happy to; they just need reminding.

## 9. Be reachable

Answer the phone or call back fast, and offer WhatsApp for customers who would rather message. The easier you are to reach, the more of those views become booked jobs.

## Where to start

Pick the leak that is costing you most. For most tradies that is the gap between being seen and being called, so start by getting a clear [profile page](/sign-up) live, then layer reviews and fast quoting on top.
